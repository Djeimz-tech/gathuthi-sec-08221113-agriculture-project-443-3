Project Overview

This portfolio documents the KNEC Agriculture Project 443/3 by Gathuthi Secondary School, showcasing student work from January to July 2025. It highlights the dedication and practical skills developed by our learners in various agricultural practices, guided exceptionally well by their subject teacher, Mrs. Muriuki.

The project emphasizes hands-on learning experiences that bridge theoretical knowledge with practical application, preparing students for real-world agricultural challenges.
